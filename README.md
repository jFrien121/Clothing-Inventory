# Clothing Inventory

Author: Jocelyn Friesen

> A customized system for keeping track clothing owned and when items have last been worn.

## Origin & Inspiration

For several years previously I've kept track of tops in an Excel sheet, and when they were worn. This provided value as it helped improve decision making for what to wear on a given day, ensured that tops were worn evenly, and prevented tops from being forgotten. It also caused recognition when tops were no longer desired and could be gotten rid of. 

Recently I decided to build a custom app, to expand and streamline my own system, and be able to easily customize different features. This also leaves room for growth and extra capabilities for the app to take on, so that should there be a desire to track additional things clothing related, it can be kept all within the same app. It seemed a perfect
intersection of a function that was used in my daily life and my love for developing software.

## Future Features

Currently the application only tracks tops, as that is the only thing that has been practically tracked in the past. However features could be added for pants, hats, accessories. If this were implemented, there could also be a system that could track the specific outfits worn together, and could make suggestions for fits to wear, based on what has been worn in the past.

Additional variables could be kept track of, such as how often an item is worn a year, or what day of the week it is generally worn on. Could also keep track of where the piece of clothing was obtained, or even clothing that has been gotten rid of and why.

The app could also hold a list of pieces of clothing that would be on a wishlist.
