# Clothing Inventory

Author: Jocelyn Friesen

> A customized system for keeping track of the clothing I, Jocelyn Friesen, own and when the item has last been worn.

## Origin & Inspiration

For several years previously I've kept track of my tops in an Excel sheet, and when I would wear them. I found this useful as it helped me avoid debating what I should wear
in the morning, wear all my tops fairly evenly, and prevent me from forgetting that I have certain tops. It also helped me recognize when I had tops that I no longer wanted to wear so that I could get ride of them. 

Recently I decided that I wanted to build a custom app, so that I could enable my own system, and be able to easily customize different features. This also leaves room for growth and extra capabilities for the app to take on, so that should I desire to track additional things clothing related, I can keep it all within the same app. It seemed a perfect
intersection of a function that I used in my daily life and my love for developing software.

## Future Features

Currently the application only tracks tops, as that is the only thing I've practically used in the past. However features could be added for pants, hats, accessories. If this were implemented, there could also be a system that could track the specific fits worn together, and potentially make suggestions for fits to wear, based on
what has been worn in the past.

Additional variables could be kept track of, such as how often an item is worn a year, or what day of the week it is generally worn on. Could also keep track of where I got the piece of clothing, or even clothing that I have gotten rid of and why.

The app could also hold a list of pieces of clothing that I'd like to get.
